SGBA induction programme for the new boats

The induction program provides members with the opportunity to learn how to access, care for and get the best out of the new boats. Most of the time will be on the water with an expert helm supporting as you put the boat through the manoeuvres required of RYA PB1 (and 2 if you want to).  See summary of standard below.

A fee of £10 is payable at booking. (Make cheques for payment to SGBA) which will be refunded on supply of a copy your RYA PB1 or PB2 certificate valid at the time of the induction.

Book an induction course via the Bosun or Quartermaster.

Induction Program to the Outhill Fox and the Yamaha 20hp engine

The induction will be in groups of 3 and take 1-1.5 hrs. Delegates will be expected to demonstrate knowledge of tide height calculations, management of shallow water, basic navigation and IRPCS and DHNA by-laws relevant to small craft operation.

  1. Preparation of boat and crew - Features, and operating procedures, controls, trim adjustment, fuel system, start up and shut down, equipment, conditions and usage
  2. Boat handling - Picking up moorings, coming alongside, bow to mooring, anchoring, standing off, tide and windage, tight manoeuvres and wash
  3. Close down - SGBA moorings, engine and equipment checks, security. How to book the boats and terms of use

See RYA PB1 and PB2 course standards available in RYA Powerboat log book G20/40
